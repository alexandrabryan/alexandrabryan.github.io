The Daily Uplift - An Email Marketing Project

A sample email marketing MVP, built remotely in a one week sprint. See The Daily Uplift in action

Project Overview

A sample email newsletter was assigned to utilize the best practices within email development and construct a sample MVP. This was our first project in the Moms Can Code School. With my partner, we decided to focus our newsletter on uplifting, positive stories that would provide our audience with a quick, daily digest of the news stories not often seen circulating the web.
We also tested our MVP for responsiveness using MailChimp.
