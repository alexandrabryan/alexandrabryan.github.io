About

I'm a Ruby on Rails web developer, inspired to create build software that makes a difference. I strongly believe that the best software comes from a foundation of empathy and human connection, because the most impactful programs and apps are the ones that draw people together and connect a shared experience.

I recently graduated from the Moms Can Code School, a coding and professional development program created specifically for moms looking to transition into development roles. I'm also a certified secondary English teacher, holding a B.A. in Journalism and Communications from Rowan University and a M.A. in Teaching from California University of Pennsylvania.
